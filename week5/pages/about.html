About Us

  • Mission Statement

    To provide UNC students with an opportunity to learn basic, real-world coding and digital-creative practices through web development.

  • Why We Exist

    1. UNC does not currently have an active website development club
    2. This is a valuable skill and a process that does not require background experience in coding
    3. We will help other clubs create websites

  • What We Offer

    Our members receive a semester of education followed by an invitation join the main club development team to make websites for other organizations and clubs:

  • How We Contribute

    • We teach topics that are important in the real world but under-emphasized in a typical college education, such as digital branding and content dissemination
    • We offer a semester of education that introduces students to different aspects of web development, from writing HTML and CSS to understanding best practices for search engine optimization and accessibility standards
    • We support other organizations by designing and implementing their websites
